EARLY COMPUTATION

1950s-1980s

The era of pioneering giant room-sized computers to the birth of the personal computer. From ENIAC to Apple II, this period laid the fundamental building blocks for our digital future. Major trends included miniaturization, increasing processing power, and the dawn of personal computing.

1990s-2010s

From secret hubs to open-sourced networks, this era showed the birth of the modern internet and mobile revolution. The World Wide Web transformed how we connect, share information, and conduct business, while smartphones changed how we interact with technology on a daily basis.

World Wide Web (1991)
