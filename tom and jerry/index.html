Characters

Get to know the beloved characters that have made Tom and Jerry a timeless classic

🐱 Tom Cat

A determined blue-grey house cat who is forever trying to catch Jerry Mouse. Despite his predatory instincts, Tom often shows a tender side and occasionally teams up with Jerry against common enemies.

🐭 Jerry Mouse

A clever brown house mouse who always manages to outsmart Tom's elaborate traps. Jerry is quick-witted, resourceful, and has a big heart, often helping other animals in need.

🐕 Spike

A tough but loving bulldog who serves as both an obstacle and occasional ally. Spike is fiercely protective of his son Tyke and often gets caught in Tom and Jerry's chaos.
